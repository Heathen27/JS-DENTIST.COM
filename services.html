PREVENTIVE CARE

  • dental cleanings: proffessional cleanings remove plaque and tarta buildup,preventing gum disease and cavities
  • Dental Exams: Regular check-ups allow dentists to assess oral health, detect issues early,and provide preventive advice
  • Dental X-Rays:X-Rays help dentists diagonise hidden dental problems such cavities,impacted teeth,and bone loss.
  • Fluoride Treatment:Application of fluoride helps strengthen tooth enamel,reducing the risk of cavities
  • Dental Sealants:Sealants are thin coatings applied to the chewing surfaces of teeth to prevent decay in grooves and pits

    • COSMETIC DENTISTRY

  • Teeth Whitening:Proffessional Whitening treatments lighten teeth and remove stains,enhancing smile aesthetics
  • Dental Veneers:Thin shells of porcelain or composite material bonded to the front of teeth to improve appearance by correcting color,shape,or size
  • Dental Bonding:Tooth-colored resin material applied to repair chipped,cracked,or discolored teeth.
  • Clear aligner trays gradually straighten teeth without metal braces,offering a more discreet orthodntic treatment option.

    • RESYORATIVE DENTISTRY

  • Dental Fillings: Restoration of decayed teeth using materials such as amalgam or tooth-colored coposite resin.
  • Dental crowns: Caps placed over damaged or weakened teeth to retore strength,shape,and appearance.
  • Dental Bridges:Fixed prosthetic devices used to replace missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to adjacent natural teeth or dental implants
  • Dental Implants: Titanium posts surgically implanted into the jawbone to serve as artificial tooth roots for supporting dental crowns,bridges,or dentures

    • SPECIALISED TREATMENT

  • Endodontics:Root canal therapy to treat infected or damaged tooth pulp,preserving the tooth and alleviating pain.
  • Periodontics:Diagnosis and treatment of gum disease,including scaling and root planing,gum surgery,and periodontal maintenance.
  • Orthodontics:Correction of misaligned teeth and jaws using braces,aligners,or other orthodontic appliances.
  • Oral surgery: Extraction of impacted wisdom teeth ,dental implants placements,and treatment of oral lesions or injuries

  • Digtal X-Rays:Faster,more efficient,and lower radiation digital imaging technology for diagnosing dental
  • intraoral cameras:small cameras used to capture images of the inside of the mouth,aiding in diagosis and patient education
  • CDA/CAM Technology: Computer-aided design and manufacturing for creating same-day restorations like crowns and bridges.
